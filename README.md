## The Core Insight

Instagram drives more purchase intent than any social platform. 70% of users discover products on Instagram. Only 13% actually buy.

**The gap is friction, not intent.**

TikTok Shop closed this gap and generated $20B GMV in its first full year. They did it by making commerce native to the content — not a separate destination.

Instagram has everything TikTok Shop has, plus:
- Better AI (Meta's vision models are world-class)
- Better personalization (Meta knows your social graph, not just your watch history)  
- Better brand safety (TikTok's counterfeit problem is a real opening)
- More users (2B vs TikTok's 1.5B)

It just hasn't built the product yet.

---

## The Feature: ShopReel

**One line:** AI identifies every product in a Reel, surfaces purchase options natively, completes checkout in 2 taps without leaving Instagram.

**Five components:**
1. AI Product Scanner — computer vision on every Reel at upload time
2. Native Product Shelf — swipe-up without pausing the video
3. Creator Commerce Dashboard — transparent commission earnings
4. Purchase Personalization — right product, right price point, right moment
5. Live Commerce — real-time product identification during Instagram Live (Phase 2)

**Target:** Reel-to-purchase conversion 1.2% → 5%, GMV $4B → $15B
